Flexibility and Mobility Exercises

Enhance your flexibility and mobility

Exercise Description Video
Yoga Yoga is a practice that combines physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to improve flexibility, strength, and mental well-being.
Pilates Pilates focuses on core strength, flexibility, and overall body awareness through controlled movements and breathing techniques.
Stretching Stretching exercises improve flexibility and range of motion by lengthening muscles and reducing muscle tension.
Foam Rolling Foam rolling is a form of self-myofascial release that helps reduce muscle tightness and improve flexibility by applying pressure to targeted areas with a foam roller.
